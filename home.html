WHY DO WE NEED TO LEARN ABOUT OUR PETS HEALTH?



Animal health and welfare are essential for ethical, environmental, economic, and societal well-being. Treating animals humanely reflects moral responsibility, while healthy animals reduce the risk of zoonotic diseases and ensure food security and quality.


Proper care minimizes economic losses, supports sustainable farming, and conserves ecosystems. Additionally, animal welfare enhances human well-being through emotional connections with companion animals. Prioritizing animal welfare benefits both animals and humans, fostering a more sustainable and humane world.
